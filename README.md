Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard – Project Explanation

I developed a Customer Behavior Analysis Dashboard using Microsoft Power BI to analyze customer purchasing patterns, spending behavior, product performance, and sales trends. The dashboard provides interactive visual insights that help businesses understand customer preferences and make data-driven decisions.

Objective

The main objective of this project was to:

Understand customer buying behavior
Identify top-performing products and categories
Analyze revenue trends across demographics and locations
Improve business decision-making using data visualization
Key Features of the Dashboard
1. KPI Overview

The dashboard includes important KPIs such as:

Total Customers
Total Spend
Average Customer Spend
Average Review Rating
Unique Products

These KPIs provide a quick summary of overall business performance.

2. Customer Demographic Analysis

I analyzed customer behavior based on:

Gender
Age Group
Location

This helps identify which customer segments contribute the most revenue.

Example:

Customers aged 51–70 generated the highest revenue.
Male customers contributed slightly higher spending compared to other groups.
3. Product & Category Analysis

The dashboard shows:

Revenue by Product Category
Top Purchased Items
Best and Worst Rated Products

Insights:

Electronics generated the highest revenue.
Shirts and shoes were the most purchased products.
Phones had lower customer ratings, indicating improvement opportunities.
4. Seasonal & Shipping Analysis

I analyzed:

Revenue by Season
Revenue by Shipping Type

This helps understand:

Peak sales seasons
Preferred shipping methods

Insights:

Spring, Winter, and Summer had the highest sales.
Standard and Express shipping were most preferred by customers.
5. Payment Method Analysis

The dashboard compares customer spending across:

Debit Card
Credit Card
Cash
PayPal
Bank Transfer

Insight:

Debit Card was the most commonly used payment method.
Tools & Technologies Used
Microsoft Power BI
Excel / CSV dataset
Data Cleaning & Transformation using Power Query
DAX Measures for KPIs and calculations
Interactive charts, slicers, and filters
Business Impact

This dashboard helps businesses:

Identify high-value customers
Improve product strategy
Optimize marketing campaigns
Understand customer preferences
Make faster and data-driven decisions
